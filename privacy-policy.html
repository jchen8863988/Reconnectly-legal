Privacy Policy — Reconnectly

Last updated: 13 May 2026  |  Effective date: 13 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the operator of the mobile application Reconnectly (“Reconnectly”, the “App”) processes information when you install and use the App. Reconnectly is operated by an independent developer (referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”). This policy is designed to meet common App Store transparency expectations and to reflect how the App is built today: local-first reminders and notes, without a first-party account system and without our own cloud database for core App features.

Contact (privacy & data requests):
Email: hello@familymedvault.com

1. Scope

This policy applies to the Reconnectly App distributed on Apple platforms (including the iOS/iPadOS versions made available through the Apple App Store) and, where applicable, other distribution channels that ship the same codebase. It does not apply to third-party websites, payment flows, or policies operated solely by Apple or other companies (see Section 6).

2. Summary (plain language)

  • Local-first core experience: Relationship reminders and contact notes you enter are primarily stored on your device (local database/storage), not uploaded by us to our servers for core App functionality—because we do not operate a first-party backend for those features in the current App version.
  • No chat reading: The App does not request access to your messages, call logs, email inboxes, or other chat apps to “read conversations.”
  • No private-content analysis / no “AI scanning” of your life: The App does not upload your contact list to an AI provider for analysis as part of the App’s functionality.
  • Pure reminder system: Reminders are based on information you manually record in the App (for example cadence, dates, and notes you choose to save).
  • Optional notifications: If you enable notifications, the device may use platform services to deliver scheduled reminders.
  • Subscriptions: Purchases are processed by Apple; we do not receive your full payment card number from Apple as part of standard in-app purchase processing.
  • No third-party analytics SDK is integrated in the current App codebase we ship for Reconnectly’s main navigation experience (as of the Last updated date above). If this changes in a future version, we will update this policy before collection begins.

Note for regulators & reviewers: Where Apple processes data as an independent platform provider (payments, App Store distribution, push transport), Apple’s privacy notices also apply.

3. Categories of information we process

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following categories of information on-device and/or through platform providers as described below.

3.1 Information you provide in the App

  • Contact-style relationship records you create (for example name fields, relationship labels, cadence preferences, notes, topics, and important dates you choose to store).
  • App settings you configure (for example language/theme preferences, notification opt-in state, and subscription entitlement flags stored locally for UI gating).
  • Backup files if you export data: the export contains what you already stored in the App (you control where it is shared or stored).

3.2 Automatically generated / technical information (device-local)

  • Operational data needed for the App to run on your device (for example local timestamps, local scheduling metadata for reminders).
  • Localization-related inputs used by the platform/localization libraries to display the App in your chosen language where supported.

3.3 Purchase-related information (via Apple)

  • If you subscribe, Apple processes payment and subscription eligibility. We may receive limited transaction metadata through the platform’s in-app purchase APIs (for example product identifiers and subscription state signals) to unlock features in the App.

4. Sources of information

  • You, when you type or select information in the App.
  • Your device, when the App stores information locally or schedules reminders.
  • Apple, when you purchase or restore subscriptions, or when platform services are used to deliver notifications.

5. Purposes of processing & legal bases (EEA/UK/Switzerland users)

If data protection laws such as the GDPR/UK GDPR apply to you, we describe our bases below at a high level. Because the App is operated by an independent developer and is primarily local-first, most processing occurs on your device to perform the contract with you (providing the App you requested) and/or based on your consent where the law requires it (for example optional notifications).

  • Provide core App functionality (local storage of your entries; generating reminders from your entries): performance of a contract / legitimate interests (operating and securing the App), depending on context.
  • Optional push notifications: typically consent where required (you can turn them off in App settings and/or system settings).
  • In-app subscriptions: performance of a contract (processing necessary to deliver purchased features) and compliance with applicable commerce rules.
  • Communications with you when you email us: legitimate interests (responding to requests) and/or performance of a contract as applicable.

We do not use your manually entered relationship notes to train third-party AI models through the App’s functionality as described in this policy for the current version.

6. How information is shared

  • We do not sell your personal information and we do not “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are commonly used in U.S. state privacy laws, as part of the App’s own functionality in the current version.
  • Apple: Apple processes data related to App distribution, in-app purchases/subscriptions, and platform services. Apple acts as an independent business in those contexts.
  • No data brokers: We do not sell contact lists to data brokers.
  • Legal & safety: We may preserve or disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect rights, safety, and integrity (to the extent reasonably necessary and proportionate).

7. Retention

  • On-device content you create remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the App, or use App features that remove/replace data (for example import flows that replace local data, if you choose to use them).
  • Emails you send us are retained only as long as needed to respond and handle your request, unless a longer period is required by law.
  • Subscription records on Apple’s side are retained according to Apple’s policies and your Apple ID purchase history.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, including a local-first architecture that reduces centralized exposure. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device (passcode/biometrics), OS updates, and any backups you choose to export.

9. Your privacy rights & choices

9.1 All users (practical controls in the App)

  • Edit or delete information you stored in the App using the App’s screens (where available).
  • Notifications: toggle in App settings (where provided) and in your device’s system settings.
  • Subscriptions: manage/cancel through Apple’s subscription management UI for iOS.
  • Uninstall: removing the App may delete locally stored App data depending on platform behavior; review your device backup settings.

9.2 EEA / UK / Switzerland

You may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, subject to applicable law. 11. International users & transfers

Apple may process purchase-related data globally in accordance with Apple’s policies. If you are in the EEA/UK/Switzerland and transfers occur, we rely on appropriate safeguards where required (for example standard contractual clauses for email support processing, where applicable).
